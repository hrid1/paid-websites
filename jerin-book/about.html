About Us

Welcome to Book Market, your number one source for buying and selling books. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of new and used books, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2024 by Jerry Afroj, Book Market has come a long way from its beginnings in a home office. When John first started out, his passion for providing the best books for his fellow readers drove him to quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the book industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Sincerely,

Jerry Afroj, Founder

Frequently Asked Questions

Book Market is an online platform where you can buy and sell both new and used books. We offer a wide variety of genres to suit all types of readers.
To buy a book, simply browse our listings, add your desired books to the cart, and proceed to checkout. We accept various payment methods for your convenience.
To sell a book, create an account, list your book with a detailed description and price, and wait for a buyer to purchase it. We handle the transaction and notify you when the book is sold.
